# Gas Management System

## Overview

The Gas Management System is a web-based application developed in Core PHP to simplify and automate gas distribution and inventory operations. The system leverages QR code technology to uniquely identify and track gas cylinders, ensuring efficient management and reducing manual errors.

## Features

* **QR Code-Based Cylinder Tracking**

  * Assign and scan QR codes for quick cylinder identification and tracking.

* **Daily Billing System**

  * Automatically generate and manage daily billing records.

* **Monthly Reports**

  * Generate detailed monthly reports to analyze sales, revenue, and operational performance.

* **Interactive Dashboard**

  * View real-time statistics, sales summaries, inventory status, and business insights through an intuitive dashboard.

* **Inventory Management**

  * Manage gas cylinder stock, customer information, and transaction records efficiently.

* **Reporting & Analytics**

  * Access comprehensive reports to support business decisions and performance monitoring.

## Technology Stack

* **Backend:** Core PHP
* **Database:** MySQL
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap
* **Additional Technology:** QR Code Integration

## Benefits

* Reduces manual data entry and operational errors.
* Improves inventory tracking and management.
* Automates billing and reporting processes.
* Provides real-time business insights through an interactive dashboard.
